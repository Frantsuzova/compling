CompLing Seminar

June 23-26, 2025

International Seminar Computational Linguistics (CompLing-2025) is focused on discussing cutting-edge research and applied developments in the fields of computational modeling and natural language processing, information retrieval, automated knowledge extraction from text corpora, and speech technologies.

A key task of the seminar is the integration of scientific approaches in computational linguistics with machine learning techniques and neural network models.


Thus, the seminar serves as a platform for interaction among computational linguists, AI researchers, and NLP industrial projects, stimulating interdisciplinary exchange of knowledge and innovations.
